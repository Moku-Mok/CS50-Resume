God, I love and hate them...

There is an ungodly amount of high-production-value garbage out there, and I really wish that I could be less cynical about one of my favourite things in the world, but especially in the circle of higher budget videogames, there has been a lot of horrible stuff going on.
From downright exploiting passionate workers in the industry, to all the manipulative tactics that go into the design of gameplay loops, the facilitating of addiction, the pushing of gambling into modern games through Lootboxes and Gacha, I just hate it all man, there's too much to talk about.

So instead, I basically exclusively play weird indie titles with even stranger premises. I really want to MAKE one of those one day, just an experience that someone will treasure for having had, a game that someone ends up loving for one reason or another.
